Tofu Scramble

An plant based alternative to scrambled eggs

Ingredients

  • Silken Tofu
  • 1/4 - 1/2 tsp of Kala Namak
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • Cracked black pepper
  • Sauerkraut juice

Method

  1. Heat up oil or butter in a frying pan on medium-high heat. Adding the silken tofy when hot.
  2. Stir, breaking up the tofu.
  3. Add spices and cook for 7-10 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in sauerkraut juice, and plant-based youghurt for extra creaminess.
